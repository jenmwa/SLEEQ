ABOUT

SLEEQ

SLEEQ hard seltzer is a pure hard seltzer made in Sweden.
SLEEQ hard seltzer is alcoholic sparkling water mixed with natural flavors.
We believe in good times and a pure experience (in both taste and in life).

SLEEQ comes in 33cl cans with abv 4.5% and every flavor is Vegan Friendly.
83kcal per can (25kcal per 100ml), no carbs (<0.5g per 100ml) and no added sugar or sweeteners.
Gluten & lactose free and we use only natural flavors.

We think that a hard seltzer should be pure, elegant and real!
(and of course presented in a (sleek) can...)

Serve SLEEQ ice cold straight from the can!
